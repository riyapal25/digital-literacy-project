# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's
1. Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles updated and professional
2. Share your academic achievements, projects, and certifications
3. Follow and engage with professionals and researchers in your field
4. Use privacy settings to control who sees your personal posts
5. Maintain a positive and respectful tone in all your posts

## ❌ Don'ts
1. Don't share personal information like phone number or address publicly
2. Don't post negative comments about your college, professors, or classmates
3. Don't use social media during lectures or exams
4. Don't share unverified news or misinformation online
5. Don't accept friend/follow requests from unknown people
