# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

<h3>General Online Safety</h3> 
<ul>
<li> Use strong, unique passwords for every account</li>
<li> Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all apps</li>
<li> Never click on suspicious links received via SMS,
      WhatsApp, or email</li>
<li> Always verify the sender before opening any attachment</li>
<li> Log out of accounts on shared or public devices</li>
<li> Keep your phone and apps updated regularly</li>
</ul>

<h3>Financial / UPI Safety</h3>
<ul>
<li> Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone —
      not even bank officials</li>
<li> Always double check the receiver's UPI ID before
      transferring money</li>
<li> Remember — receiving money NEVER requires
      entering your PIN</li>
</ul>

### If you face any cybercrime — Report Immediately
📞 Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7) <br>
